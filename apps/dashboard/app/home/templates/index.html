What is Lymphoblast Counter ?

Lymbhoblast Counter for Assisting Hematologist Initial Screening on Diagnosing Leukemia.

Hematologists use manual microscopic examination for initial screening because it is the most economical way (for now) with potential human-errors of 30-40% and subjective reports [8,10].

There are numbers of consequences that possible to appears including:

  • Human-error increasing with time due to fatigue [10].
  • Manual examination would be bias and subjective reports [8].

    • These consequences above deduce to the following questions:

  • Would machine learning approach improve hematologists initial screening ?
  • Which approach be the most economical way ?

    Project Objective

    Incorporate machine learning to be able to help hematologist for initial screening on diagnosing leukimia.

    The machine learning model that we proposed is able to facilitate doctors, hematologists, or other medical officers, in order to classifying blast and nonblast cell correctly. We use pre-trained SSD ResNet50 V1 FPN 640x640 (RetinaNet50) model and ALL-IDB public limited dataset as our training data.
